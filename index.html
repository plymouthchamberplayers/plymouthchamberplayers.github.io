Plymouth Chamber Players

Passion and Pathos: Defining Romanticism in Chamber Music

June 20, 2023

Featuring international prize-winner violinist Joshua Brown, Plymouth Chamber Players explores the full scale of human emotions expressed through romanticism. Works by Brahms, Mendelssohn, and Kreisler showcase variety and nuance within the Romantic period.
